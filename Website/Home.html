About us

We are Team 2035, a FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team from Carmel, California known as the Rockin' Bots. was established in 2007 at Carmel High School by Paul McFarlin and Tom Clifford as a club with just a small group of students. Since its start, the team has been growing and currently has more than 30 participating students.

The team partakes in the annual FIRST (For Inspiration and Research of Science and Technology) Robotics Competitions. The students enjoy learning real life useful applications such as coding, wiring, designing, and engineering. Our focus has expanded beyond science as well; we now have students working on business, project management, communications, art, and team spirit.

About FIRST

For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology (FIRST) is a non-profit organization founded by Dean Kamen in 1989 with the goal of inspiring young people to pursue an education in STEM-related fields. Through competition, cooperation, and gracious professionalism, FIRST reaches students throughout the United States and the world. You can find more information at firstinspires.org.
